ML Algorithms Used

Two powerful algorithms trained and compared

Linear Regression

Finds the best-fit linear relationship between 30+ engineered features and each target variable. Regularised variants (Ridge, Lasso, ElasticNet) are also evaluated for optimal bias-variance tradeoff.

  • CGPA R² = 1.000
  • Stress R² = 0.996
  • Depression R² = 0.885

K-Nearest Neighbours (KNN)

Non-parametric instance-based learner that predicts values by distance-weighted averaging of the k closest training samples. K is auto-tuned via 5-fold cross-validation.

  • Auto-tuned k per target
  • Distance-weighted voting
  • CGPA R² = 0.806
